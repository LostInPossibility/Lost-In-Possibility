Lost In Possibility

"I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me."
Philippians 4:13


The songs for Lost In Possibility are written by Phillip Coomer Jr a Christian who began writing biblically inspired songs after a 20 year break in songwriting and creating music.

Now with advances in technology, where otherwise he would not be able to produce songs, Phillip uses artificial intelligence to carefully prompt and style the music, beats, and voice in the songs to create Lost In Possibility.

The songs of Lost In Possibility are meant to be a warning to Christians. Jesus said in Luke 17:26-27 and Matthew 24:36-39 that "As it was in the days of Noah, so also will it be in the days of the Son of Man...Then the flood came and destroyed them all."

We are approaching those days.

About Me

Phillip Coomer Jr


Christian, Father, Husband, Son, Brother, Veteran

After 2020 I began to embrase my dormant Christianity. I wasn't lukewarm. I had lost my spark. At my wife's behest I began attending our local church. At a friends recommendation I began listening to Christian podcasts like Bible Mysteries and Blurry Creatures. Now I am on fire and trying to find my way to serve. I was inspired to begin writing songs again after seeing a waning moon that looked like bull horns. I thought about idol worship under the bull-moon's light in the days of Noah, and wrote a song as a warning about where we are now in humanity. I found an AI platform to help me make the music for my lyrics, 90s themed as I used to be. Now I am writing more biblically inspired songs that will hopefully be a bridge to those who need it.
